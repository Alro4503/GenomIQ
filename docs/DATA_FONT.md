# DATA SOURCES

## Sequencing Databases
- **NCBI GenBank** - Database of DNA and RNA sequences from different organisms.  
  https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/ 

- **UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot** - Collection of protein sequences with detailed annotations.  
  https://www.uniprot.org/ 

## Protein Databases and 3D Structure
- **Protein Data Bank (PDB)** - Information on three-dimensional structures of proteins and RNA.  
  https://www.rcsb.org/

- **AlphaFold Protein Structure Database** - AI-generated protein structure predictions.  
  https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk/

## Databases for Alignment and Sequence Comparisons
- **BLAST (NCBI)** - Rapid comparison of sequences against public databases.  
  https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Blast.cgi
